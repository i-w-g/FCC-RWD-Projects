Kobe Bryant: a tribute

Kobe Bryant is a former NBA superstar, who played his entire career (20 years) for the Los Angeles Lakers. He leaves behind a legacy that represents hard work, loyalty, respect, and dedication to his craft.
  • 5x NBA Finals Champion
  • 2x NBA Finals MVP
  • 18x NBA All Star
  • 11x All-NBA First Team
  • 9x All-NBA Defense First Team
  • 2x NBA Scoring Champion
